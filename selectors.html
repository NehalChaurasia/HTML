SOME BASIC LAWS

Human Trafficking

Prostitution in or in the vicinity of public place:

1.)Any person who carries on prostitution and the person with whom such prostitution is carried on in premises such as public religious worship,educational institute,hotel,hospitalor any public places must be notified by the police or magistratein the manner prescribed.And shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which may extend to three months.

2.) When the offence in committed with respect to the child or minor ,theperson committing the offence shall be punishable with imprisonment for not less than 7 years but which may be for the life time.
